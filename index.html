Welcome to DCSE Department

The Department of Computer Science Engineering (DCSE) at Government Polytechnic Warangal focuses on delivering high-quality technical education, practical skills, and industry-ready training.

About the Department

DCSE is dedicated to producing competent diploma engineers with strong programming, problem-solving, and system design skills. The department emphasizes real-world applications, internships, and emerging technologies.

Core Subjects

  • Programming in C & C++
  • Python Programming
  • Data Structures
  • Database Management Systems
  • Web Development
  • Operating Systems

Vision

To develop skilled and innovative computer engineers capable of contributing to the IT industry and society.

Mission

  • Provide strong technical foundation in computing.
  • Encourage innovation and project-based learning.
  • Prepare students for industry and higher education.
